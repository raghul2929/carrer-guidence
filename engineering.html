Computer Science Engineering

A Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Computer Science is an undergraduate degree program that provides a comprehensive education in computer science and technology. Students in this program study programming, algorithms, software engineering, computer architecture, and various other aspects of computer science. This degree prepares graduates for careers in software development, IT consulting, data analysis, cybersecurity, and related fields, as well as offering a strong foundation for further studies in computer science at the graduate level if desired.

Detailed Values
Branch Name Computer Science Engineering
Degree B.E,M.E
Duration B.E/B.Tech - Four years, M.E -2 Years
Admission Process Entrance exam and counselling process
Course Fees Rs.5 Lakh to Rs. 10 Lakh for the entire course
Average Salary INR 6 lakhs
Career Options Software developer, CSE Engineer, Web developer, Hardware Engineer
Top Recruiting Companies TCS,Wipro,Infosys,Cognicant,Microsoft,Tech Mahindra,Facebook,Adobe,Twitter,HCL..,

Information Technology

Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Artificial Intelligence, often abbreviated as AI, refers to the development of computer systems and software that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include understanding natural language, recognizing patterns, solving problems, learning from experience, and making decisions.

Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that involves the collection, analysis, interpretation, and presentation of data to extract valuable insights, support decision-making, and solve complex problems. Data scientists use a combination of skills in statistics, programming, data engineering, and domain knowledge to work with large and diverse datasets.

Computer Science and Business System Engineering (TCS)

Computer Science and Business System Engineering (TCS) is a dynamic field that seamlessly blends the power of computer science with the intricacies of business systems. In this interdisciplinary realm, students delve into the core principles of computer science, honing their skills in programming, data management, and software development. Simultaneously, they gain a deep understanding of business processes, including operations, supply chains, and quality control. TCS professionals are uniquely positioned to bridge the technological divide in the business world. They analyze complex business challenges and craft innovative solutions that leverage cutting-edge technologies. This skill set encompasses database management, project management, and a keen ability to align IT strategies with overarching business objectives. As the digital landscape continues to evolve, TCS experts are indispensable, driving efficiency and competitiveness in a wide range of industries..

EEE encompasses a wide range of areas, including electrical power generation and distribution, electrical circuits, control systems, telecommunications, electronics, and digital and analog systems. EEE engineers work on developing and improving systems that generate, transmit, and use electrical power, as well as electronic devices and systems that process information, control machines, and enable modern technologies like computers, smartphones, and automation systems.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is a branch of engineering that focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure and facilities essential for modern society. This field encompasses a wide range of projects, including buildings, bridges, roads, tunnels, dams, airports, water supply and sewage systems, and more. Civil engineers are responsible for ensuring that these structures are safe, functional, sustainable, and environmentally sound. They work on various aspects of a project, from planning and design to construction and maintenance, with a primary goal of improving and enhancing the infrastructure that supports communities and economies.

Chemical Engineering

Chemical engineering is a branch of engineering that applies the principles of chemistry, physics, mathematics, and biology to design, develop, and optimize processes and systems for the production, transformation, and utilization of chemicals, materials, energy, and related products. Chemical engineers work on a wide range of projects, including the design and operation of chemical plants, the development of new materials and processes, and the improvement of existing industrial processes to maximize efficiency, safety, and sustainability. They play a crucial role in various industries, such as pharmaceuticals, petrochemicals, food processing, energy production, and environmental management.

MotorSport Engineering

Motorsport engineering is a captivating blend of cutting-edge technology, mechanical prowess, and high-speed adrenaline. It revolves around the art and science of crafting race cars that push the limits of performance on the track. Engineers in this field are tasked with the daunting challenge of designing vehicles that are not only incredibly fast but also safe, reliable, and finely tuned to respond to the slightest driver input. From optimizing aerodynamics to enhancing powertrains, every aspect of a race car's design is meticulously scrutinized. Motorsport engineers are at the forefront of innovation, constantly seeking new ways to shave off fractions of a second, fine-tune handling, and ensure driver safety in the most demanding racing environments.
