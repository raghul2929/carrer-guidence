What are Paramedical Courses?

Paramedical courses refer to educational programs that train individuals to provide medical support services to patients and assist medical professionals in diagnosis, treatment, and therapy. These courses are designed to equip students with the necessary knowledge and skills to work in various healthcare settings, such as hospitals, clinics, and emergency medical services.
