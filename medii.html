MBBS is the core course pursued by students who want to become doctors professionally, during MBBS subjects such as clinical, para clinical and preclinical are studied. This undergraduate program is of 5 years duration and 6 month internship. In order to pursue this course students have to qualify the NEET exam, which is an all India pre-medical entrance examination.,

Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery (BAMS) :


Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery (BAMS) is an undergraduate degree program in the field of alternative medicine, specifically Ayurveda. Ayurveda is a traditional system of medicine that originated in India and focuses on holistic healing and natural remedies. BAMS is a comprehensive program that combines the study of ancient Ayurvedic principles, herbal medicine, and therapeutic practices. Graduates of BAMS are trained to diagnose, treat, and prevent various health conditions using Ayurvedic methods and principles, which include herbal formulations, dietary recommendations, and lifestyle adjustments. This degree enables individuals to become Ayurvedic doctors and practitioners, providing healthcare services based on the principles of Ayurveda.

Bachelors of Vocational Training (OTT):


Paramedical courses present ample employment opportunities because of the expansion of the healthcare industry. Paramedical courses are job-oriented skilling programs that transform an individual learner into a competent healthcare worker. Paramedical courses give rise to allied healthcare professionals who deliver direct patient care, rehabilitation, treatment, diagnostics, and health improvement interventions to restore minimal optimal functions in an individual. B.Voc in OTT is a 3 year duration program that includes internship in it’s course duration to get hands-on training experience in top hospitals.
